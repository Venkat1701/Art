ABOUT GENEVA

Background

When I was in preschool, my favorite time of day was when I was allowed to draw and paint. I try to keep that sense of exploration and play alive in my work.

Medium

I use mainly acrylics and pastels. My latest projects have expand into clay as well. Each idea manifests in its own individual style.

Inspiration

I enjoy hiking in my spare time and many of my pieces are directly influenced by these hikes in nature. I love recreating plants and wildlife in my work.
